About Us

Ecstatic Cleaners is your trusted partner in maintaining a clean, safe, and hygienic environment. Based in Lagos, Nigeria, we specialize in laundromat, residential, commercial, and post-construction cleaning services. Our team is committed to delivering top-notch cleanliness, using advanced tools and techniques. With a focus on professionalism and customer satisfaction, we help you enjoy a healthier, more comfortable living and working space.

We also dabble into Cold Water Liquid Starch Making, Fumigation and Pest Exterminantion, as well as Rug Cleaning services. Our expertise in cleaning hazardous areas in commercial and residential facilities, along with consistent training in the use of specialized cleaning tools and equipment, sets us apart.

Our team of experienced professionals is committed to delivering excellence in every cleaning project. We take pride in our attention to detail and our use of eco-friendly cleaning products. Discover the joy of hassle-free cleaning with Ecstatic Cleaners.
